FAQ

CoderRank is the ultimate quiz app designed to help aspiring programmers challenge their coding skills and climb the ranks.

To participate in quizzes, simply sign up for an account, log in, and browse through the available quizzes. Click on a quiz to start solving questions.

Currently, only administrators have the ability to create quizzes. However, users can suggest quiz topics by contacting us.

Quiz rankings are determined based on the number of correct answers and the time taken to complete the quiz. Users with higher scores and faster completion times rank higher.

Yes, CoderRank is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges. Simply sign up and start participating in quizzes.

The score of any quiz will only update to the backend when the quiz is taken for the first time. Subsequent attempts will not update the score to ensure fair ranking and prevent abuse of the system.
